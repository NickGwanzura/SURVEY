# Zimbabwe RAC Technician Registry — User Guide

**Website:** https://racregistryzw.org
**Audience:** RAC/HVAC-R technicians, retailers, wholesalers, distributors, and importers in Zimbabwe
**Cost:** Registration is completely **free**. No account or login is required.

---

## 1. What Is This App?

The Zimbabwe RAC Technician Registry is a national programme run by the **National Ozone Unit (NOU)** and **HEVACRAZ** to map and support refrigeration and air-conditioning (HVAC-R) technicians and businesses across all 10 provinces of Zimbabwe.

**By registering, you help Zimbabwe:**

- Design better training programmes for HVAC-R technicians
- Comply with international Montreal Protocol requirements
- Track access to safer, modern refrigerants
- Build a credible national workforce record

**What you get:**

- A **reference number** confirming your registration
- The option to be listed in the **National Technician Directory**
- Access to training invitations and communications from NOU/HEVACRAZ (with your consent)

## 2. Before You Start — What You Will Need

Have the following ready:

| Item | Required? |
|---|---|
| Full name (as on your national ID) | ✅ Required |
| Zimbabwe phone number in international format (e.g. `+263771234567`) | ✅ Required |
| Your province, city/town, and suburb/area | ✅ Required |
| Details of your training and certifications (if any) | ✅ Required (answer "No" if none) |
| Email address (to receive confirmation and updates) | Optional |
| A clear profile photo of your face (max 2 MB) | Optional |
| GPS location | Optional |

**Phone number format:** remove the leading `0` from your local number and add `+263`.
Example: `0771234567` → `+263771234567`

## 3. Registering as a Technician

### Step-by-step

1. Open your phone or computer browser and go to **https://racregistryzw.org**
2. Tap **"Start the survey"**

The survey has **6 steps**. A progress indicator at the top shows where you are (e.g. *Step 2 of 6*).

#### Step 1 — Background Information

Your personal and location details:

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| First Name / Surname | As on your national ID (letters only) |
| Gender | Male / Female / Prefer not to say |
| Age Group | Select your bracket |
| Education Level | Your highest qualification |
| Years of Experience | How long you have worked in HVAC-R |
| Main Work Focus | The types of HVAC-R work you do (select more than one if needed) |
| Province, City/Town, Suburb | Where you mainly work |
| GPS Location | Optional — tap "Use my location" or pin on the map |
| Phone Number | `+263` followed by 9 digits |
| Email Address | Optional |

#### Step 2 — Skills and Training

- Whether you have formal HVAC-R training, where you trained, and the year you qualified
- Certifications held (or "Currently Studying"); HEVACRAZ membership number if you are a member
- Self-ratings on a **1–5 scale** (1 = Very Low, 5 = Very High) for: confidence with traditional refrigerants (R22, R134a…), confidence with low-GWP refrigerants (R32, R290, R600a), and how easily you can access tools, spare parts, and low-GWP refrigerants

#### Step 3 — Tools and Resources

Rate how much these obstacles affect your work (1 = Not an obstacle, 5 = Severe obstacle):

- High import costs for parts and equipment
- Forex shortages
- Unreliable suppliers
- Counterfeit or substandard products

You may also describe other obstacles in your own words.

#### Step 4 — Work Challenges

- Your biggest daily challenge
- How often load shedding (power cuts) affects your work
- Whether you use refrigerant recovery equipment
- Your access to PPE (personal protective equipment)
- Your biggest barriers to EHS (Environment, Health, Safety) compliance

#### Step 5 — Energy Efficiency

- How often you install energy-efficient equipment
- If not "Always": what stops you (tick all that apply)

#### Step 6 — Consent and Photo (final step)

- **Consent to be contacted** — choose Yes to receive training opportunities and industry updates from NOU/HEVACRAZ
- **Consent to public registry listing** — choose Yes to appear in the public National Technician Directory. Only your **name, province, and certification status** are ever shown publicly — **never** your phone number or email
- **Preferred language** for future communications: English, Shona, or Ndebele
- **Profile photo (optional)** — take a photo or upload one from your device (max 2 MB)
- **Data protection notice** — you must tick the consent checkbox to submit

Tap **"Submit Registration"**.

### After Submitting — Your Reference Number

You will see a confirmation screen with your **reference number** — a short 8-character code, for example:

```
Reference: A3F2B1C4
```

> ⚠️ **Write this number down or take a screenshot.** You need it, together with your phone number, to edit your registration later.

If you provided an email address, a confirmation email arrives within a few minutes.

## 4. Editing Your Registration

If you need to correct something after submitting:

1. Go to **https://racregistryzw.org**
2. Tap **"Edit an existing application"**
3. Enter the **phone number** you registered with **and** your **reference number** (from your confirmation screen or email)
4. Tap the look-up button — your form loads pre-filled with your current answers
5. Correct any fields and submit again

**Lost your reference number?** Contact NOU or HEVACRAZ with your name and phone number.

---

## 5. Saving and Resuming a Draft

The survey **saves your progress automatically** on your device as you go. If you close the browser or lose connection:

1. Return to https://racregistryzw.org
2. Tap **"Start the survey"**
3. You will be offered the chance to continue your saved draft

> **Note:** Drafts are saved on your device only. If you switch to a different phone or clear your browser data, the draft will not be there.

---

## 6. Using the App Without Internet (Offline Mode)

The app works offline on most modern smartphones:

1. The first time you visit, the app saves itself to your phone (like installing an app)
2. If prompted to **"Add to Home Screen"**, tap **Add** — you can then open it from your home screen like a normal app
3. You can complete the whole survey without wifi or mobile data
4. Your submission is stored on your phone and **sent automatically** the next time you have internet access

If you see an orange **"Working Offline"** banner, this is normal — your answers are safe and will be submitted when you are back online.

---

## 7. Registering as a Retailer / Distributor

If you are a **retailer, wholesaler, distributor, or importer** of RAC equipment:

1. Go to **https://racregistryzw.org/retailer-survey**
2. Complete the **4-step** business survey:
   - **Step 1 — Business Information:** business name, type, location, size
   - **Step 2 — Products and Sourcing:** what you sell and where you source it
   - **Step 3 — Challenges:** supply challenges, load shedding impact, training interest
   - **Step 4 — Consent:** contact consent and data protection agreement
3. Submit and note your **reference number**, just like technicians (Section 3)

Draft-saving and offline mode (Sections 5–6) work for the retailer survey too.

## 8. Privacy and Your Data

Full details: https://racregistryzw.org/privacy-notice

**What we collect:** your name, contact details, location, professional background, survey answers, and (optionally) your profile photo.

**How we use it:** to build a national HVAC-R workforce database, design training programmes, report to UNEP under Zimbabwe's Montreal Protocol obligations, and (with your consent) contact you about training.

**Who can see it:**

- Only NOU and HEVACRAZ staff can see your full record
- If you consented to the public directory: only your name, province, and certification badge appear on the website
- Your phone number and email are **never** shown publicly

**Your rights:** request a copy of your data, correct it (use *Edit an existing application*), request deletion, or withdraw consent at any time — contact NOU or HEVACRAZ.

## 9. Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I have to pay to register?**
No. Registration is completely free.

**My phone number is not in the +263 format. What do I do?**
Remove the leading 0 and add +263. Example: `0712345678` → `+263712345678`.

**I don't have an email address. Can I still register?**
Yes. Email is optional; your phone number is your primary identifier.

**I don't have GPS on my phone.**
GPS is optional. Pin your approximate location on the map, or skip it.

**I made a mistake after submitting.**
Use *Edit an existing application* on the home page with your phone number and reference number.

**I registered before. Do I need to register again?**
No — edit your existing application instead. Duplicate registrations are flagged.

**Will my information be used for enforcement?**
No. Data is collected for workforce planning and training, not regulatory enforcement.

**How long until my registration is verified?**
NOU/HEVACRAZ staff review submissions. There is no fixed timeline; you will receive an email notification when your status changes (if you gave an email address).

**I lost my reference number.**
Contact NOU or HEVACRAZ with your name and registered phone number.

**Can I complete the survey in Shona or Ndebele?**
The survey is currently in English; you can choose Shona or Ndebele as your preferred language for future communications. Contact NOU/HEVACRAZ if you need help completing the survey.
