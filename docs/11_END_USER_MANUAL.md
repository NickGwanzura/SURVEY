# END USER MANUAL
## Zimbabwe RAC Technician Registry — Registration Guide

**Document Reference:** NOU-HEVACRAZ-UM-2025-001
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 2025-06-01
**Language:** English
**Audience:** RAC Technicians, Retailers, and Distributors across Zimbabwe

---

## WELCOME

The Zimbabwe RAC Technician Registry is a national programme by the **National Ozone Unit (NOU)** and **HEVACRAZ** to map and support refrigeration and air-conditioning (HVAC-R) technicians across all 10 provinces.

**By registering, you help Zimbabwe:**
- Design better training programmes for HVAC-R technicians
- Comply with international Montreal Protocol requirements
- Track access to safer, modern refrigerants
- Build a credible national workforce record

**What you get:**
- A reference number confirming your registration
- A chance to be included in the **National Technician Directory**
- Access to training invitations and communications from NOU/HEVACRAZ

---

## PART 1 — HOW TO REGISTER (TECHNICIANS)

### Step 1: Access the Registration Survey

Open your phone or computer browser and go to:

```
https://racregistryzw.org
```

You will see the registration homepage.

**You do NOT need to create an account or log in.**

---

### Step 2: What You Will Need

Before starting, have ready:
- ✅ Your **full name** (as on your national ID)
- ✅ Your **Zimbabwe phone number** in international format (e.g., +263 77 123 4567)
- ✅ Your **province, city/town, and suburb/area**
- ✅ Details about your **training and certifications** (if any)
- ✅ Optional: Your **email address** (to receive a confirmation and future communications)
- ✅ Optional: A **profile photo** (a clear photo of your face)

---

### Step 3: Start the Survey

Click the green button **"Start the survey"**.

The survey has **6 steps**. You can see which step you are on at the top of the screen (e.g., Step 2 of 6).

---

### STEP 1: Background Information

Fill in your personal and location details:

| Field | What to Enter |
|---|---|
| **First Name** | Your given name (2–50 characters, no numbers) |
| **Surname** | Your family name |
| **Gender** | Male / Female / Prefer not to say |
| **Age Group** | Select your age bracket |
| **Education Level** | Your highest qualification |
| **Years of Experience** | How long you have worked in HVAC-R |
| **Main Work Focus** | Select the types of HVAC-R work you do (can select more than one) |
| **Province** | Your home province |
| **City / Town** | The city or town where you mainly work |
| **Suburb / Area** | Your suburb or local area |
| **GPS Location** | Optional — tap "Use my location" or pin on the map |
| **Phone Number** | Your Zimbabwe phone number: +263 followed by 9 digits |
| **Email Address** | Optional — enter if you want to receive communications |

**Zimbabwe Phone Number Format:**
- Your number must start with **+263**
- Example: If your number is **0771234567**, enter it as **+263771234567**
- Remove the leading 0 and add +263

Click **"Next"** when done.

---

### STEP 2: Skills and Training

Tell us about your qualifications:

| Question | How to Answer |
|---|---|
| **Do you have formal HVAC-R training?** | Yes or No |
| **If Yes: Where did you train?** | Name of school or institution |
| **If Yes: What year did you qualify?** | Enter the year (e.g., 2019) |
| **Do you hold a certification?** | Yes, No, or Currently Studying |
| **If Yes: Which certifications?** | Tick all that apply |
| **If HEVACRAZ member: Membership number** | Enter your HEVACRAZ number |

Then rate yourself on a **scale of 1 to 5** for:
- Confidence working with traditional refrigerants (R22, R134a, etc.)
- Confidence working with low-GWP refrigerants (R32, R290, R600a)
- How easily you can access tools and equipment
- How easily you can access spare parts
- How easily you can access low-GWP refrigerants

**1 = Very Low, 5 = Very High**

---

### STEP 3: Tools and Resources

Rate how much these obstacles affect your work (1 = Not an obstacle, 5 = Severe obstacle):

- High import costs for parts and equipment
- Forex shortages (difficulty getting foreign currency)
- Unreliable suppliers
- Counterfeit or substandard products

Optionally, describe any other obstacles.

---

### STEP 4: Work Challenges

Answer these questions about your day-to-day work:

1. **Biggest daily challenge** — Select from the dropdown list
2. **How often does load shedding (power cuts) affect your work?** — Select frequency
3. **Do you use refrigerant recovery equipment?** — Select how often
4. **Do you have access to PPE (personal protective equipment)?** — Select your situation
5. **What are your biggest barriers to EHS (Environment, Health, Safety) compliance?** — Tick all that apply

---

### STEP 5: Energy Efficiency

1. **How often do you install energy-efficient equipment?** — Select one option
2. If not "Always": **What stops you?** — Tick all barriers that apply

---

### STEP 6: Consent and Photo (Final Step)

This is the last step before submitting.

**Consent to be contacted:**
> Select **Yes** if you agree to be contacted by NOU or HEVACRAZ for training opportunities, surveys, or industry updates. Select **No** if you prefer not to be contacted.

**Consent to public registry listing:**
> Select **Yes** if you agree to have your name and province appear in the public RAC Technician Directory on the website. Only your name, province, and certification status will be shown publicly — never your phone or email.
> Select **No** if you prefer your details remain private.

**Preferred Language:**
> Select your preferred language for future communications: English, Shona, or Ndebele.

**Profile Photo (optional):**
> Tap "Take Photo" to use your camera, or "Upload Photo" to select a photo from your device. A clear face photo helps verify your identity. Maximum size: 2MB.

**Data Protection Notice:**
> Read the data protection notice carefully. **You must tick the consent checkbox to submit.** This confirms you have read and agreed to how your data will be used.

Click **"Submit Registration"** when you are ready.

---

### Step 4: Submission Complete

You will see a **confirmation screen** with your **Reference Number** — for example:

```
Reference: a3f2b1c4-...
```

**Write this number down or take a screenshot.** You may need it if you want to edit your registration later.

**If you provided an email address**, you will receive a confirmation email within a few minutes.

## PART 2 — EDITING YOUR REGISTRATION

If you need to correct something after submitting:

1. Go to `https://racregistryzw.org`
2. Click **"Edit an existing application"**
3. Enter your **Zimbabwe phone number** (the same one you registered with)
4. Click **"Look up my application"**
5. Your form will be pre-filled with your current answers
6. Correct any fields
7. Submit again

---

## PART 3 — SAVING AND RESUMING

The survey **automatically saves your progress** as you go. If you close the browser or lose connection:

1. Return to `https://racregistryzw.org`
2. Click **"Start the survey"**
3. You will be asked if you want to continue your saved draft

**Important:** Your draft is saved on your device only. If you use a different phone or clear your browser data, the draft will not be there.

---

## PART 4 — COMPLETING THE SURVEY WITHOUT INTERNET (OFFLINE MODE)

The survey can work even without an internet connection on **most modern smartphones**.

**How it works:**
1. The first time you visit the survey, the app is saved to your phone (like installing an app)
2. You will see a prompt asking "Add to Home Screen" — tap **Add** to install it
3. Next time, open it from your home screen like a normal app
4. Complete the survey even without wifi or data
5. Your submission is saved on your phone and sent automatically when you next get internet access

**Orange banner:** If you see an orange banner saying **"Working Offline"**, your submission will be saved and sent later. This is normal.

---

## PART 5 — RETAILER REGISTRATION

If you are a **retailer, wholesaler, distributor, or importer** of RAC equipment:

1. Go to `https://racregistryzw.org/retailer-survey`
2. Complete the 4-step business survey:
   - Step 1: Business information (name, type, location, size)
   - Step 2: Products and sourcing (what you sell, where you source it)
   - Step 3: Challenges (supply challenges, load shedding, training interest)
   - Step 4: Consent

## PART 6 — PRIVACY AND DATA PROTECTION

Your privacy is important to us. Here is what you need to know:

### What data we collect
- Your name, contact information, and location
- Your training, certification, and professional background
- Your responses to all survey questions
- Optional: your profile photo

### How we use it
- To build a national database of HVAC-R technicians in Zimbabwe
- To design training programmes that meet your needs
- To report to UNEP under Zimbabwe's Montreal Protocol obligations
- To contact you about relevant training opportunities (with your consent)

### Who can see your data
- **Only NOU and HEVACRAZ staff** can see your full record
- If you consented to the public registry: your name, province, and certification badge are shown on the public website
- Your phone number and email are **never shown publicly**

### Your rights
You have the right to:
- Request a copy of your data
- Correct incorrect data (use the Edit Application function)
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact NOU or HEVACRAZ.

## PART 7 — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Do I have to pay to register?**
A: No. Registration is completely free.

**Q: My phone number is not in the +263 format. What do I do?**
A: Remove the leading 0 from your local number and add +263 at the start. For example: 0712345678 becomes +263712345678.

**Q: I don't have an email address. Can I still register?**
A: Yes. Email is optional. Your phone number is the primary identifier.

**Q: What if I don't have GPS on my phone?**
A: GPS is optional. You can use the map to pin your approximate location, or skip GPS entirely.

**Q: What if I submit and then realise I made a mistake?**
A: Use the "Edit an existing application" option on the home page. You will need your registered phone number.

**Q: Will my information be shared with the government for enforcement purposes?**
A: No. Data is collected for workforce planning and training, not regulatory enforcement.

**Q: I registered before. Do I need to register again?**
A: No. Use the "Edit an existing application" option to update your information instead.

**Q: How long before my registration is verified?**
A: NOU and HEVACRAZ staff review submissions. There is no fixed timeline, but you will receive an email notification when your status changes (if you provided an email).

**Q: I lost my reference number. What do I do?**
A: Contact NOU or HEVACRAZ with your name and phone number.
